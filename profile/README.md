## What is Rocket.Chat?

Modern organizations require secure communication platforms that support team collaboration, customer engagement, operational coordination, and digital workplace productivity. As concerns around privacy, compliance, vendor lock-in, and data ownership continue to grow, many organizations are seeking self-hosted alternatives to commercial communication platforms. Rocket.Chat provides a powerful open-source communication and collaboration platform that enables organizations to maintain full control over their communication infrastructure.

Rocket.Chat is one of the most popular self-hosted messaging platforms used by enterprises, government agencies, educational institutions, healthcare providers, technology companies, and security-conscious organizations worldwide. The platform combines internal team collaboration, customer communication, omnichannel engagement, and operational workflows within a unified environment.

At its foundation, Rocket.Chat provides real-time messaging through channels, private groups, direct messages, threaded conversations, file sharing, notifications, and searchable communication history. Teams can collaborate efficiently while maintaining ownership of their infrastructure and data.

Many organizations deploy Rocket.Chat as an alternative to cloud-based collaboration platforms because it offers extensive customization, flexible deployment options, strong security controls, and open-source transparency. Self-hosted deployments allow businesses to meet regulatory requirements while maintaining complete control over communication systems.

Rocket.Chat supports omnichannel customer engagement by integrating multiple communication channels into a centralized workspace. Organizations can manage customer conversations from websites, live chat widgets, social platforms, messaging services, and support channels through a unified interface.

Live chat functionality is one of Rocket.Chat's most widely used features. Businesses deploy website chat widgets to provide customer support, sales assistance, onboarding guidance, and real-time engagement while maintaining ownership of customer interactions and communication data.

Video conferencing and collaboration capabilities can be expanded through integrations such as Jitsi. These integrations enable teams to conduct meetings, support sessions, project discussions, incident response activities, and remote collaboration directly within communication workflows.

The platform supports enterprise-grade authentication and security controls including LDAP, Active Directory, SAML, OAuth, single sign-on, role-based permissions, auditing capabilities, compliance management, and advanced governance policies. These features make Rocket.Chat suitable for highly regulated industries.

Deployment flexibility is a major advantage of Rocket.Chat. Organizations deploy the platform using Docker containers, Kubernetes clusters, cloud environments, virtual machines, on-premises infrastructure, Linux servers, Windows environments, and hybrid architectures.

Rocket.Chat Cloud provides managed hosting options for organizations that prefer reduced infrastructure management while retaining access to enterprise collaboration features. Self-hosted deployments remain popular among organizations prioritizing privacy, customization, and infrastructure ownership.

Developers frequently integrate Rocket.Chat with DevOps pipelines, monitoring systems, ticketing platforms, workflow automation tools, customer relationship management systems, and business applications. APIs and extensibility features allow organizations to customize communication workflows according to operational requirements.

Whether supporting internal collaboration, customer service operations, secure communication, omnichannel engagement, digital workplaces, project coordination, or enterprise messaging, Rocket.Chat provides a flexible and scalable platform for modern communication needs.
